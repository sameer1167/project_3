MineralTurpentine Oil

Mineral Turpentine Oil is otherwise called as mineral spirits or white spirits, MTO is distilled from petroleum which is a clear liquid known by the nameâ€™s petroleum spirit, solvent naphtha and mineral turpentine. A blend of aliphatic and alicyclic C7 to C12 hydrocarbons, the white sprit is utilized as an extraction dissolvable, as a cleaning dissolvable, as a degreasing dissolvable and as a dissolvable in mist concentrates, paints, Lacquers, Aerosols, varnishes and enamels.

Uses:

  • Industrial cleaning
  • Blending
  • Aerosols and Lacquers
  • Wood preservatives
  • Paints and Varnishes
  • Dry cleaning
  • Rubber solvent agent
